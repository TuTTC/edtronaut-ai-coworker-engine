# 360° Feedback & Coaching Program Guide

## Classification: Internal — HR Team

### 1. Program Objectives

- Provide leaders with multi-source feedback on competency strengths and development areas
- Create a structured coaching engagement to accelerate leadership growth
- Build a data-driven view of leadership capability across the Group
- Support talent mobility decisions with objective assessment data

### 2. 360° Instrument Design

#### 2.1 Rater Groups
| Group | # Raters | Mandatory? |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Self | 1 | Yes |
| Direct Manager | 1 | Yes |
| Direct Reports | 3-5 | Yes |
| Peers (same brand) | 2-3 | Yes |
| Peers (cross-brand) | 1-2 | For mobility candidates |
| External (clients/partners) | 1-2 | Optional |

#### 2.2 Scale
- 5-point behavioral frequency scale: Never / Rarely / Sometimes / Often / Almost Always
- Plus: "Unable to assess" option

#### 2.3 Items per Theme
- 4-6 items per competency theme (Vision, Entrepreneurship, Passion, Trust)
- Total: 16-24 items + 2 open-ended questions
- Estimated completion time: 15-20 minutes per rater

#### 2.4 Anonymity & Confidentiality
- All rater responses are anonymous (except direct manager)
- Minimum 3 responses per rater group to show results (prevents identification)
- Results shared only with the individual and their coach

### 3. Coaching Program Structure

#### Phase 1: Assessment (Weeks 1-2)
- Complete 360° feedback instrument
- Coach reviews results and prepares debrief

#### Phase 2: Debrief & Goal Setting (Week 3)
- 90-minute debrief session with certified coach
- Identify 2-3 development priorities
- Co-create Individual Development Plan (IDP)

#### Phase 3: Coaching Sessions (Weeks 4-16)
- 6 × 60-minute coaching sessions (bi-weekly)
- Focus on practicing new behaviors in real situations
- Mid-point check-in on IDP progress

#### Phase 4: Re-assessment (Month 6)
- Shortened 360° pulse survey (same items, same raters)
- Progress report generated
- Development plan updated

### 4. Vendor Decision: Build vs. Buy

| Factor | Build | Buy |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Customization | Full control | Limited by vendor platform |
| Cost (Year 1) | High (development + testing) | Medium (license + config) |
| Cost (Ongoing) | Low (maintenance) | Medium (annual license) |
| Time to Launch | 6-9 months | 2-3 months |
| Data Ownership | 100% in-house | Depends on contract |
| Recommendation | Long-term play | Quick pilot for Wave 1 |
